# raghavk.github.io

About
This is my personal portfolio site, built to showcase how I think about products, technology, and strategy. It brings together my work across product management, AI/ML, finance, and a range of interests that shape how I approach problem-solving — from chess and poker to music and basketball.
The site is designed to be more than a static resume. It's a living portfolio where I publish product teardowns, technical projects, strategic analyses, and long-form articles that explore ideas across disciplines.
What's Inside

Interactive N-Body Gravity Simulator — a real-time physics playground where visitors can spawn celestial bodies, launch them with velocity, and watch gravitational attraction, orbital mechanics, and collisions unfold. Built from scratch with HTML5 Canvas and vanilla JavaScript.
Product Projects — end-to-end case studies and builds including PRDs, competitive analyses, product teardowns, and technical prototypes — each framed through a product management lens with clear problem statements, user research, design rationale, and success metrics.
Articles — essays exploring the intersections of product thinking with game theory, decision science, AI, finance, and more. Topics range from expected value frameworks borrowed from poker to team composition lessons drawn from basketball analytics.
Interest Map — a visual overview of the domains I actively study and how each one informs my approach to building products.

Physics Simulator
The gravity playground implements an N-body gravitational simulation with:

Newtonian gravitational attraction between all bodies
Elastic collision detection and momentum-conserving mergers
Configurable mass via scroll input
Velocity vectors via click-and-drag launch mechanics
Orbital trail rendering with gradient fade
Gravitational field line visualization between nearby bodies
Touch support for mobile devices
