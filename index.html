About

I'm a product manager who believes the best products come from people who think broadly and build deeply. My background spans technology, finance, and a lifelong curiosity about how things work — from algorithms to musical harmony to market microstructure.


I use this portfolio to build in public — shipping projects, writing about ideas, and documenting what I learn along the way. Every project here is framed through a product lens: who's the user, what's the problem, and how do we measure success?
